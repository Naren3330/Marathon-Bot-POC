FRESHWORKS CHENNAI MARATHON 2026 - REGISTRATION FAQ

================================================================================
REGISTRATION OPTIONS
================================================================================

Question: What are the various registration options?

Answer: You can register online by visiting www.thechennaimarathon.com. The website provides a secure online registration system where you can select your race category, fill in your details, and complete payment.


================================================================================
BULK AND CORPORATE REGISTRATIONS
================================================================================

Question: How do I register for bulk or corporate registrations (more than 25 people)?

Answer: For bulk or corporate registrations of more than 25 participants, please write to us via the Contact Us form on our website or send an email directly to corporateregistration@chennairunners.in. Our team will assist you with the bulk registration process and provide special corporate registration support.


================================================================================
PAYMENT CONFIRMATION ISSUES
================================================================================

Question: I made the payment online but did not receive a confirmation email. What should I do?

Answer: If you have made an online payment but haven't received a confirmation email, please email info@thechennaimarathon.com with your payment details and transaction date. If your payment is registered in our system, we will resend the confirmation email to you. 

Important: If you use Gmail, please check your Promotions tab as confirmation emails sometimes land there instead of your primary inbox.


================================================================================
BIB NUMBER GENERATION
================================================================================

Question: I received the confirmation email but my BIB number is not generated. Is this normal?

Answer: Yes, this is completely normal. This year, BIB numbers are generated only after the closure of registration, not immediately upon payment. You will receive a reference number which you can use for any communication with us. Your BIB number will be sent to you closer to race day, well before the event.


================================================================================
CHANGING RACE CATEGORY
================================================================================

Question: Can I change my race category after registration?

Answer: Details about changing race categories will be updated shortly. Please visit our website www.thechennaimarathon.com regularly to check for updates on this policy.


================================================================================
CANCELLATION AND REFUND POLICY
================================================================================

Question: What is the cancellation and refund policy?

Answer: We understand that unforeseen circumstances might force you to withdraw from the race. However, unfortunately no cancellations or refunds will be entertained once registration is complete. 

Exception: In case your application gets rejected by the Race Organizers for any reason, a full refund will be initiated (bank charges applicable). 

All registrations are final and non-refundable, so please be certain before registering.


================================================================================
REGISTERING FOR OTHERS
================================================================================

Question: Can I register for a friend or family member?

Answer: Yes, you can register multiple people in a single transaction. During registration, you can choose the number of participants you want to register for each category and add all attendee details. You can also use the "Add Runner" option to register your friends or family members.

Important: When registering on behalf of others, please ensure you:
- Fill in their correct details accurately
- Provide accurate medical information for each participant
- Understand that you are accepting the Health Declaration and Waiver on their behalf
- Inform them about the medical information and declarations you've submitted


================================================================================
RACE KIT CONTENTS
================================================================================

Question: What will I receive after registering for the marathon?

Answer: After registration, you will receive the following:

Before Race Day (at Expo):
- Running number bib with electronic timing chip (mandatory - without this you cannot participate)
- Official race T-shirt
- Goodie bag with sponsor items

On Race Day:
- Baggage handling facility
- On-course hydration at aid stations
- Medical support throughout the route
- Great running experience with fellow runners

After Finishing:
- Finisher's medal (for those completing within cutoff time)
- Hearty breakfast at the finish line

The bib and timing chip are essential - do not lose them!


================================================================================
BIB COLLECTION BY REPRESENTATIVE
================================================================================

Question: Can someone else collect my BIB and race kit on my behalf?

Answer: Yes, you can authorize a friend, colleague, or family member to collect your BIB and Marathon kit on your behalf. 

The representative must bring the following documents:
1. An authorization letter signed by you (the participant) directing us to hand over the kit to the representative
2. A photocopy of the representative's valid ID proof - any one of these:
   - Driving License
   - Passport
   - School/College Identity Card
   - PAN Card
   - Voter ID Card
   - Aadhar Card

Without these documents, we cannot release the kit to the representative.


================================================================================
TRANSPORTATION FROM FINISH POINT
================================================================================

Question: Will there be transportation back from IMU (India Maritime University) to the start line?

Answer: Yes, limited buses will be available at the India Maritime University (IMU) finish point for transporting runners back to the start line. Please look for transportation signages at the finish point in IMU. These buses are available on a first-come, first-served basis.

Note: Limited transport buses will be available specifically for Half Marathon, Twenty Miler, and Full Marathon finishers who finish at IMU, Uthandi.


================================================================================
RACE DAY KIT COLLECTION
================================================================================

Question: Can I collect my race kit on race day instead of the Expo?

Answer: No, you cannot collect your BIB and race kit on race day. All participants must collect their kits at the designated Expo before race day. 

Details of the Expo including:
- Expo dates and timings
- Expo location and address
- What to bring for kit collection

...will be shared shortly via email and on our website. Please plan accordingly and collect your kit during the Expo period.


================================================================================
REGISTRATION TRANSFER POLICY
================================================================================

Question: Can I transfer my registration to a friend or family member?

Answer: No. Freshworks Chennai Marathon 2026 registrations are strictly non-transferable. Once you register, the registration is tied to your name and cannot be transferred to another person under any circumstances. 

If you cannot participate, the registration fee is also non-refundable (see Cancellation Policy above).
